Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This is the best recipe for Chicken Noodle Soup.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Chicken broth
  • Chopped cooked chicken breast
  • Egg noodles

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender
  2. Add chicken broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, salt and pepper.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
